Charity Water Catcher

Catch clean water droplets, avoid dirty ones, and keep going until you run out of lives. This prototype matches your mockup and is ready to customize for your charity app.

How to play

Move the canister left and right with the arrow keys or by moving your mouse.

Blue droplets increase your score.

Black droplets remove a life.

3 lives to start Blue = good Black = bad
